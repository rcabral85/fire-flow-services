# Pre-Job Checklist (Onsite Prep)

Use this checklist before starting any NFPA 291 fire flow testing work.

## Planning
- [ ] Client intake complete and PO received
- [ ] Hydrant list imported and zones defined
- [ ] Traffic control plan reviewed (OTM Book 7)
- [ ] Notifications to stakeholders sent (fire, dispatch, public works)

## Equipment
- [ ] Pitot gauges calibrated and documented
- [ ] Nozzles and caps sized and inspected
- [ ] Hoses, wrenches, and keys loaded
- [ ] PPE and safety signage packed
- [ ] Spill kit and first aid kit onboard

## Site Readiness
- [ ] Water quality impacts assessed (flushing plan)
- [ ] Nearby sensitive facilities noted (hospitals, schools)
- [ ] Access and parking confirmed
- [ ] Weather and temperature suitable

## Data & Reporting
- [ ] Mobile devices charged and synced
- [ ] Test forms/templates loaded
- [ ] Camera/GPS verified
- [ ] Backup paper forms available
